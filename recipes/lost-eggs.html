Lost Eggs

Description

This hearty breakfast dish features savory creamed spinach & soft baked eggs hidden under a blanket of smoked ham, havarti cheese & crispy breadcrumbs. For a vegetarian version; substitute seasoned and roasted oyster mushrooms

This recipe is from Broder in Portland, OR. Which I can attest makes this at an extremely high level, that simply does not skip a beat

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Butter
  • 8 oz of Julienne cut Yellow Onion
  • 1 tablespoon of Minced Garlic
  • 1/2 cup of White Wine
  • 1 pint Heavy Cream
  • 2 tablespoons Prepared Horseradish
  • 1/3 cup of Shredded Parmasean
  • 8 oz of Washed Baby Spinach
  • 6 whole eggs
  • 1/3 cup of Shredded Havarti Cheese
  • 4 oz of Thinly Shaved Ham
  • 1 cup Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 1 cup of Shreded Paramsean Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a medium skillet on medium heat until melted
  2. Add onions, cook until wilted
  3. Add garlic, salt and pepper
  4. Add white wine, cook until wine is reduced by half
  5. Add heavy cream and lower to a simmer. Cook for 10 minutes on low heat to thicken the cream
  6. Preheat oven to 375degrees
  7. Add Horseradish and 1/3 cup of Parmasean Cheese
  8. Once cheese has melted, turn off heat an add spinach
  9. Stir until spinach is wilted
  10. Adjust seasoning as needed
  11. Transfer creamed spinach to a cast iron skillet
  12. Crack whole eggs over the mixture, sprinkle with shredded Havarti Cheese
  13. Place folded shaved ham in an even layer over the eggs.
  14. Combine Parmasean and panko bread crumb, sprinkle over the ham evenly until covered but not too thick. You should still see a little bit of the ham showing
  15. Place in oven @ 375 degrees. Bake until the crust is golden brown, approximately 15 - 20 minutes
