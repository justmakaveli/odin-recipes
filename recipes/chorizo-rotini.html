Chorizo Rotini

Description

Inspired by an Instagram post with a single image and a detailess description with little more than the title. Amy was inspired and we went from there to recreate the dish.

In the two times we've made it since we've not used rotini, and our ricotta was expired before we could use it. Beware soft cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 tube of chorizo (pork or beef is fine, I perfer pork)
  • 16 oz of pasta (we use penne or mafaldine)
  • 1 cup of red pasta sauce (you could substitute crushed tomatoes)
  • Ricotta on hand as topping
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Vaguley italian seasoning or fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Begin by cooking your pasta until soft and chewy a.k.a Amy Style
  2. Chop garlic finely
  3. While your pasta boils, brown your chorizo and garlic in a pan
  4. Add your sauce or tomatoes to your chorizo without draining the fat
  5. Drain and rinse your pasta then toss in the pan with your chorizo-sauce
  6. Plate pasta, garnish with vaguely italian seasoning or fresh herbs
  7. Top with pasta to personal prference
  8. Enjoy!
