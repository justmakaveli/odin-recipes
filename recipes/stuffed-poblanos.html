Southwest Stuffed Poblano Peppers

Description

Stuffed poblano peppers are a simple and easy meal to make on weekdays, even if you forget to cook the rice beforehand!

Poblano peppers have plenty of kick to add more flavor to the dish, without overhwelming sensitve palettes.

This recipe is adapted from Le Creme de la Crumb, in our recipe we will be using ground turkey to reduce the amount of fat

Ingredients

  • 4 large green poblano peppers
  • 1 pound of ground turkey
  • Half a cup of canned corn
  • Half a cup of canned black beans
  • 15oz of fire roasted diced tomatoes (one can is typically 15oz)
  • 4oz of hatch green chiles (these typically come in 4oz cans)
  • 1 cup of cooked long grain rice
  • Mexican cheese blend (enough to sprinkle!)
  • One teaspoon of garlic powder, chile powder, and ground cumin
